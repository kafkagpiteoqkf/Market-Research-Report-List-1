<p><p>The micro pipette market is segmented into two main types: those with air cushions and those without. Micro pipettes with air cushions utilize an air-filled chamber to create a precise volume measurement, ensuring greater accuracy and reliability in liquid handling. Conversely, micro pipettes without air cushions rely on direct piston action to dispense liquids, typically offering quicker operation but with potential for reduced accuracy. Each type serves specific laboratory applications, catering to different user needs in research and clinical settings.</p></p>

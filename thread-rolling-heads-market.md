<p><p>Thread rolling heads are essential tools in manufacturing for creating threaded components. They are categorized into three market types: fixed, rotatable, and others. Fixed thread rolling heads are designed for consistent, high-volume production, ensuring precision in thread creation. Rotatable heads offer versatility, allowing multiple thread profiles to be produced by adjusting the head position, making them ideal for varied applications. The 'others' category includes specialized or niche rolling heads tailored for specific industries or unique threading requirements, enhancing overall production efficiency.</p></p>

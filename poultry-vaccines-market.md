<p><p>The poultry vaccines market primarily includes two types: liquid vaccines and freeze-dried vaccines. Liquid vaccines are ready-to-use formulations that provide immediate immunity and are often administered through drinking water or injection. In contrast, freeze-dried vaccines are lyophilized, preserving their potency for longer shelf life but requiring reconstitution before use. Both types play crucial roles in preventing poultry diseases, ensuring healthy flock management, and enhancing production efficiency in the poultry industry. Each type serves specific vaccination strategies based on operational needs.</p></p>
